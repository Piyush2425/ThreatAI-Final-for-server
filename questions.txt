Here are good question types your dataset supports, plus feature ideas to grow the project.

Question types you can ask now

Profile/overview: “Tell me about APT28” or “Report on Sofacy”
Origin/attribution: “Where is APT28 from?” “Who sponsors Lazarus Group?”
Aliases: “What are the aliases of APT29?”
Timeline: “When was APT28 first seen?” “Last known activity of Fancy Bear?”
Targets: “Who does APT28 target?” “Which sectors are targeted by Lazarus?”
TTPs/tools (if present): “What tools does APT28 use?” “Common tactics of APT29?”
Campaigns/operations: “What notable campaigns are linked to APT28?”
Associations: “What actors are related to APT28?”
Sources: “What are the sources for APT28?”
Feature ideas to add next

Actor comparison: side‑by‑side diffs for origin, targets, tools, timeline.
Timeline view: render a concise activity timeline from first/last seen.
Confidence breakdown: show why a confidence score is high/low.
Source credibility tags: rank sources (vendor, gov, blog) and show in evidence.
Evidence clustering: group evidence by category (origin, TTPs, targets).
Entity linking: auto-expand aliases and related actors in the UI.
Query templates: one‑click questions for a selected actor.
Export improvements: JSON/CSV with structured fields (origin, last activity, etc.).
